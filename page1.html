Manhole Covers

Manhole covers are often an access point to the many systems and networks which reside below us. Underground cable systems are everywhere in Wellington, allowing cables to travel over large distances where they can not be physically interfered with or disturbed. These cables play a crucial role in the infrastructure of the internet, as it carrys binary data from our devices around the world, allowing us to make connections over these networks.
