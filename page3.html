Powerlines

Power lines play a crucial part the massive infrastructre which makes up the internet. These tall structures carry overhead cables which are used to transmit electrical energy. This electrical energy can be used to power the machinery used to convert binary data, which is ultimately what makes up the internet. These large posts also often carry the direct cable which carry this data from an ip adress to a server, allowing us to access information over the internet.
